The Process

From site visit to published review

Every step is designed to look and feel like a genuine customer journey — because it is one.

1

Athlete gets access to LG.com

The athlete is pointed to the LG website generally — not a direct link to any specific product page. There's no shortcut to the item they'll eventually buy.

2

Search & navigate like a real shopper

From there, the athlete searches for the product themselves and navigates to it like any normal customer would, generating organic-style search and browsing signal along the way.

SEO benefit
3

Purchase with their own money

The athlete buys the product with their own card at checkout. The purchase is tracked end-to-end through CJ, so LG can see the exact cost of every transaction.

Tracked via CJ
4

Product is received

The athlete receives and uses the product just like any other customer.

5

Thoughtful review, real media

The athlete writes a genuine, detailed review and includes original photo or video — no AI-generated media, ever.

6

Review is posted

The completed review is published to the product page.

7

Review approved → reimbursement

Once LG approves the review, NIL Club is reimbursed 110% of the full purchase cost, including tax — verified against the exact CJ-tracked order amount.

110% reimbursed
8

Optional: UGC + affiliate earnings

After the review is complete, the athlete can create additional UGC about the product and share a tracked affiliate link — earning ongoing commission on any sales it drives.

~100%

Completion rate, by design

Athletes purchase with their own money up front and aren't made whole until a review is submitted and approved — so finishing the job isn't a hope, it's the incentive. That's what a near-100% completion rate looks like.

Reimbursement Models

Two ways to structure the partnership

LG chooses the model per campaign — reward the review alone, or guarantee content alongside it.

Track A
110%
Standard Review

LG reimburses NIL Club 110% of the product cost (including tax) once the review is submitted and approved. UGC is optional and, if the athlete chooses to make it, is monetized separately through a tracked affiliate link.

  • Honest written review required
  • Original, non-AI photo/video required
  • UGC creation is optional, affiliate-earned
  • Full cost tracked and verified via CJ
$100 product → 110% ($110) reimbursed to NIL Club
Track B · UGC Included
150%
Review + Guaranteed UGC

LG reimburses NIL Club 150% of the product cost, and in exchange the athlete is required to also produce dedicated UGC content about the product — guaranteeing brand content, not just a review.

  • Honest written review required
  • Original, non-AI photo/video required
  • Dedicated UGC content is required, not optional
  • Full cost tracked and verified via CJ
$100 product → 150% ($150) reimbursed to NIL Club
